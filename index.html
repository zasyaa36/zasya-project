I Fall ..


I fall in love. I fell in love with the sky, I fell in love with the stars, the clouds, the moon,
I fell in love with all the beauty up there.
I think astronomy is a very elegant science, studying it made me realize that planet earth
is just a grain of dust in the power of the universe. I was enchanted by the beauty of the sky, the solar system,
the Milky Way Galaxy, the Black Hole, and the Law of Relativity.
There are still many unsolved mysteries and interesting things that I have never known.
